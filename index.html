About BuildX

Overview & Vision

BuildX was born from a vision to transform the XRPL ecosystem. Recognizing the lack of accessible analytics tools and the need for transparency in the XRP space, BuildX emerged as a solution to empower traders with precise tools and real-time insights.

Our platform combines advanced analytics, AI-driven predictions, and precision trading tools to create an unparalleled trading experience. Whether you're monitoring market trends, analyzing support and resistance levels, or optimizing your trading strategies, BuildX delivers the speed and reliability you need.

Community & Future

At BuildX, we're building more than a platform – we're fostering a community dedicated to transforming XRP trading. Our commitment to transparency, innovation, and community growth drives everything we do. Join us in creating a fairer, more transparent ecosystem for all XRPL traders.

Join our growing community on Telegram and be part of the revolution in XRPL analytics and trading. Stay updated with platform developments, airdrops, and exclusive announcements.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is BuildX?

BuildX is an advanced trading and analytics platform built on the XRP Ledger, offering real-time data, AI-driven predictions, and precision trading tools for traders of all levels.

How do I get started with BuildX?

Getting started is easy! Simply connect your XRPL wallet, explore our intuitive dashboard, and access our comprehensive suite of trading tools. Join our Telegram community for guidance and support.

What makes BuildX unique?

BuildX combines advanced analytics, AI-driven insights, and a user-friendly interface to provide a comprehensive trading experience. Our platform offers unique features like customizable dashboards, real-time market analysis, and seamless AMM integration.

How can I participate in airdrops?

Regular airdrops are happening for BuildX and other communities. To participate, join our Telegram group and follow the instructions provided. Make sure to set up your trustline early to avoid delays. All airdrop requirements and dates will be announced through our official channels.
